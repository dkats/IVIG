This website is meant to help double-check IVIG pump settings according to the MGH policy. We recommend printing the table out and taping it to the pump, so that you don't need to come back to the computer.

This website uses the following protocol: Initial rate 0.3 mL/kg/hour for the first 30 minutes. May increase by 0.3 mL/kg/hour every 30 minutes to a maximum of 2 mL/kg/hour.

Please do not rely on this website for the initial calculations; only use it for checking your work.
